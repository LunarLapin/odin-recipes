Southwest Wrap

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenders
  • Flour Tortillas
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Black Beans
  • Corn
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Milk
  • Salsa
  • Taco Seasoning

Steps

  1. Bake Chicken Strips according to instructions on package.
  2. In a small mixing bowl add mayonnaise, milk, salsa, taco seasoning, and cumin. Mix well and set aside.
  3. While chicken is baking, place thawed corn into a small skillet with 1 Tbsp. oil and cook until corn is lightly browned.
  4. Remove Chicken from the oven and cut into small strips.
  5. Start assembling your chicken wraps by adding a couple Tbsp. of the southwest dressing to the lower half of the tortilla. Add romaine lettuce, sliced tomatoes, black beans, corn, chicken strips, and drizzle more southwest dressing on top.
  6. Roll up tortilla tightly, cut in half and serve with a side of southwest dressing.
